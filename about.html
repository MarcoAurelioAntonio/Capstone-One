The Insurance Agent (PAS) is mainly an intermediary between the Insured and an Insurance Company in the execution of an insurance contract. Its main function is to provide advice to both insured and insurable before, during, and after the term of a policy, regarding the clauses and scope of each product, to provide the best solution by adjusting it to the reality of each person. All the functions and duties of the PAS are regulated by Art. 10 of Law 22,400. But our team goes further and is at the service of our partners by providing the best care.
